Information We Collect

Our plugin does not collect or store personal information from users. The plugin operates locally within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers. The only information processed by the plugin is the content of social media posts displayed on the active tab, which is necessary to generate summaries. This data is used solely for the purpose of summarizing the posts and is not stored or shared.

How We Use Your Information

We use the content of social media posts you view to generate summaries. This data is processed in real-time and is only used locally within your browser. We do not collect, store, or share any of the data processed by the plugin.

Third-Party Access

Our plugin does not share or transmit any information to third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Security

Since our plugin does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no need for external data security measures. All processing happens locally within your browser, ensuring your privacy is protected.
